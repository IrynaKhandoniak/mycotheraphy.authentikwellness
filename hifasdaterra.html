Hifas da Terra is a biotechnology company focused on developing health solutions derived from medicinal mushrooms. With over 25 years of experience, they specialize in producing organic nutraceuticals, medical devices, and pharmaceuticals aimed at treating various health conditions. The company emphasizes sustainability, research, and innovation, conducting numerous clinical trials on the benefits of their products, including cancer and digestive health. They operate internationally and are committed to quality and environmental responsibility. For more information, visit Hifas da Terra.
