(for children)

CLINICAL APLICATION

IMMUNE SYSTEM
  • General strengthening oaf the immune system
  • Most common bacterial, viral or parasitic infections: otitis, bronchitis, rhinitis
  • Seasonal colds
  • Children who are immunologically depressed who suffer from repeated infectious diseases

EFFECTS

  • Immunomodulatory
  • Antiviral
  • Antibacterial
  • Antiparasititic
  • Antifungal

TROPISMS

  • Immune System

DIRECTION FOR USE

0.50 ml/kg body weight on an empty stomach or outside of meals using the convenient dosing syringe which prevents spillage and allows for accurate dosing.

DURATION

At least 2 months. It is important to monitor the child and adapt the dosage according to the severity of their condition and the symptoms presented. Hifas de Terra recommends an ongoing maintenance dose to prevent relapses and to help optimise other treatments applied.

SAFETY

The medicinal mushroom extracts from Hifas da Terra have been shown in studies to have a very low risk of drug interaction with hepatic cytochromes responsible for metabolising conventional drugs.
